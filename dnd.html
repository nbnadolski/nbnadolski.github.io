Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop role-playing game, or TTRPG where a group of people get together, roll some dice, and have a great time. The game uses pure imagination, since one of the players must be the Dungeon Master, in which they are in charge of controlling the story. Everything they say will need to be imagined by the other players. There are many useful tools online that new players can gain access to. One of which has a very useful tutorial and instructions on how to play the game: dndbeyond.

D&D Facts

  • There are 12 different classes to play in the 5th edition Player's Handbook
  • A "typical" party consists of four members
  • There are seven different dice to use: d4, d6, d8, d10, d%, d12, and d20
